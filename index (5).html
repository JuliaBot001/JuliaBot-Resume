I am hoping my further education will provide me with ample opportunities to be able to find a sustainable job in either of these fields. I have always had a passion for understanding people and learning how to help and contain some behaviors expressed by those in times of crisis. I enjoy being a compassionate listener and am always willing to give out a helping hand and suggest what I believe to be the right thing in any given situation.

I do not have frequent free time as I am often busy taking care of myself, my school responsibilities, and my work responsibilities. I am very dedicated to anything I do, and tend to prioritize anything made out to me to be important, even if it is not as important as other things I may have going on. I always make time to help people out, even if it means I will fall behind slightly in a task for myself. I never do things out of self-benefit, and am always looking at the greater good for everyone around me. I am very friendly and hardworking, and am a very easy person to get along with in any given environment or situation. I love new experiences, working hard, being dedicated to everyone around me, and helping out others whenever possible.

I have had some past job experiences, all of which having been in a restaurant or a fast food environment. I have had much experience with assisting managers with more difficult tasks and situations, and have often been applauded for my efforts to go the extra mile in the workplace. I am a hard and determined worker, and hate to feel as though I could have done a better job in any situation. I believe one should be passionate while working, and dedicated to making the workplace a better environment for both the customers and employees alike.
